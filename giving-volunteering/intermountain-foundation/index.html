Intermountain Foundation

Building relationships that inspire generosity in support of Company_Name

Company_Name’s mission is, Helping People Live the Healthiest Lives Possible.® Intermountain Foundation builds relationships and partnerships that inspire generosity in support of Company_Name's mission.

Intermountain Foundation oversees local and regional fundraising programs within Company_Name throughout Utah and Southern Idaho. Local programs raise funds to benefit Intermountain hospitals, medical research, and other initiatives based on community need.

Consider making a charitable gift to Intermountain Foundation by making an online gift to one of our hospitals or clinics, or by participating in one of our fundraising events. To learn about other ways to make a lasting impact at Company_Name, contact one of our local foundation offices, located in Company_Name's Hospitals., to learn about other ways.
